Sustainable development

Deep roots

Moyo in Swahili means heart. It is therefore no coincidence that we put all our dedication and care into being a holistic company.

From the the first harvest, to the package that arrives at your front door, we want our product to have a positive environmentaland social impact.

To flourish & grow

We have close relationships with our local and international suppliers.

Every month we send out a little team to visit the farmer, to ensure we’ve got the best quality products that aresourced from ethical means.

We look to support small-scale farmers so that their communities may flourish.

Another purpose

Our packaging is plastic-free, made from 100% recycled paper, and with biodegradable, toxic-free inks and dyes.

When you’re done with munching on your favourite mix, use your Moyo bag as a seedling starter or as a compost bag in the kitchen. Get creative!
